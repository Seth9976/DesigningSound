Audio

Thunder sounds

Three strikes showing different levels of detail. First is a highly efficient castle thunder using only four subtractive bands, high crash, middle, mid-low rumble, sub-boom. Next is a cheap N-Wave and multi-tap delay model. Finally is a composite N-Wave and large environment model made from several multi-taps and reverbs to model different environment features.
